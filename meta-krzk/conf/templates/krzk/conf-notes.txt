
### Shell environment set up for builds. ###

You can now run 'bitbake <target>'

Common targets for distro krzk are:
    bitbake board-test-image
    MACHINE=qemuarm64 bitbake board-qcom-image
    bitbake core-image-minimal

Machines
    odroid (default): ARMv7 boards in the lab
    qemuarm64: ARM64 boards of Qualcomm Landing Team

For distro krzk-musl:
    DISTRO=krzk-musl bitbake board-test-image
